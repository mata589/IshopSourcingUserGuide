IshopSourcing User Guide

Introduction

IshopSourcing is an e-commerce platform designed to simplify the sourcing process by connecting buyers with trusted suppliers. Whether you're shopping for products or managing a store as a supplier, this app has all the tools you need.

For Buyers

Getting Started

When the app loads, you'll be directed to the Shopping Dashboard. Here's what you can do:

  1. Explore Products:
    • Promotions: View featured and discounted products.
    • Categories: Browse products sorted by categories.
    • New Arrivals: Discover the latest additions.
    • More Products: Explore the full product range.
  2. Navigate Suppliers:
    • Switch to the Suppliers Tab to view stores and suppliers.
  3. Add Products to Cart:
    • Tap on a product to open its Product Detail Page.
    • Select your preferred variations (e.g., size, color) while adhering to the minimum order quantities.
    • Add the product to your shopping cart.
  4. Initiate an Order:
    • Once your cart has items, proceed to initiate an order.
    • Order Details: Enter necessary details, including your preferred delivery address and contact information.
    • Payment: Currently, only payment on delivery is supported.
  5. Order Management:
    • Note that products from different suppliers are treated as separate orders.
    • Track the status of each order until it is delivered.

For Suppliers

Getting Started

  1. Set Up Your Store:
    • Go to the Profile Tab.
    • Tap on the Create Store option.
  2. Manage Your Store: Once your store is created, you'll have access to a Supplier Dashboard:
    • Add Categories and Products: Create product categories and add detailed product listings.
    • Add Variations: Customize products with different options (e.g., sizes, colors).
    • Promote Products: Highlight products in promotional sections for better visibility.
    • Edit Product Details: Update product descriptions, prices, and other details as needed.
    • View Analytics: Monitor store performance with insights on sales and product engagement.

Order Fulfillment for Suppliers

  • Manage orders placed by buyers through your dashboard.
  • Update order statuses to keep buyers informed.
